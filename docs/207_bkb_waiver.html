2. Express Assumption of Extreme Inherent Risks

I, the undersigned Participant, acknowledge that participating in high-intensity backcountry hiking, mountaineering, or extreme terrain exploration involves inherent risks that cannot be eliminated regardless of the care taken by the Organizers. These risks include, but are not limited to:

  • Severe weather shifts, extreme cold/heat, lightning, high winds, and avalanche/rockfall hazards.
  • Remote wilderness navigation errors, falling off steep ledges, or unstable footing on scree/ice.
  • Severe physical distress, hypothermia, dehydration, altitude sickness, animal attacks, or cardiac incidents.
  • High latency or total lack of cell service, meaning emergency medical care or search-and-rescue (SAR) may be delayed by hours or days.
No-Device Program. This is a disconnection retreat: participants voluntarily surrender all personal phones and connected devices at check-in. The lead guide carries the party's only backcountry communicator. In the event of separation, participants will have no means of personal contact.

I freely and voluntarily choose to participate, and I fully assume all such risks, including the risk of serious bodily injury, permanent disability, or death.

3. Warrant of Physical Fitness & Mandatory Equipment

Fitness: I warrant that I am in top physical condition, have undertaken appropriate training for high-altitude/extreme terrain hiking, and have no medical conditions that endanger myself or others.

Gear: I agree to carry all required safety gear specified by the Organizers (e.g., GPS beacon, microspikes/crampons, layered cold-weather clothing, extra rations, individual first-aid kits). The Organizers reserve the right to disqualify me on-site without a refund if my equipment is unsafe or inadequate.

4. Emergency Medical & Evacuation Authorization

In the event of an emergency, I authorize the Organizers to secure first aid, medical care, or emergency evacuation (via helicopter, ground SAR, or ambulance) at their discretion. I explicitly agree to assume full financial responsibility for all medical and evacuation costs incurred on my behalf.
